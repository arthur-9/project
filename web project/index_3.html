At this picture you have three different elements, these are: R, I and U
R is for resistance. I for Ampaer and the U is for Volt. At this picture
you'll notice that this triangle is for a rule for electrical engineering.
Which this rule can help you for calculate something:
U = R * I
R = U / I
I = U / R



The mesh rule is an important rule in electrical engineering. It states
that in a closed circuit (i.e. a loop) the sum of all voltages is zero.



The node rule states that at a node in a circuit, the sum of the currents
flowing into the node is equal to the sum of the currents flowing out of
the node.
