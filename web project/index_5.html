Everything About Trigonometry

Trigonometry is a type of math that looks at how the angles
and sides of triangles are connected, especially in right triangles.

What are this elements(sin, cos and tan)

  • Sine(sin): Measuring height (e.g., height of a tree using its shadow and angle).
  • Cosine(cos): Calculating horizontal distances.
  • Tangent(tan): Determining slopes or angles of elevation/depression.
